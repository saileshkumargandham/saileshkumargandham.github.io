I-Quest
A pro quizzing Event is ready to test your Quizzing skills and Show off your non-academic skills here and compare yourself with the competitive world. I-Quest doesn't just test your knowledge but also analyses your pressure handling skills and your Brain synapse power.Do you think you are great in doing these things? Then get ready. Come and check them out here at Quiz-Bee!
SCHEME OF EVENT:-
• The total Event consists of Two phases namely, Screening Level and Prolevel
• The Students shortlisted from the Screening phase are only eligible to participate In Pro-Level.
• The screening Level is completely objective based test with a specified time limit whereas the ProLevel is completely different which includes A Buzzer round with the Participants divided into Groups of 4 or 5 members.
• The Quiz questions will be included from Topics like Mythology, Politics , Automobiles, Entertainment, History, General knowledge related etc;
RULES AND REGULATIONS : -
1. This event is open to all undergraduate and post graduate students Pan India.
2. Each team should comprise of 2 members only.
3. An individual can be a part of only one registered team.
4. The decision of organising committee is final under any circumstances And Organising team has all rights in all matters related to any issue.
5. There is no cap on the number of teams registered per institute.
6. If any Individual/Team found to be violating Above Rules and Regulations , Organising Team has all rights to detain them without Intimation.
REGISTRATION FEE:-
₹ 50/- per person.
PRIZE MONEY:
Exciting prize money for the winning and Runner up team.
JUDGEMENT CRITERIA :
The team with maximum points at the final round will be declared as the winning team and the next team with highest number of points will be declared as Runner up Team.
